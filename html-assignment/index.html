About Me

Hello, Professor Robert Kingsbury! I'm happy to have the opportunity to learn from you. My name is Natalia Ribeiro Moreira, I was born on February 25th, 2004 in São Paulo, Brazil. My first language is Portuguese, I have been vegetarian for 8 years, and before coming to Canada I studied data science at college. Like you, I also had a cockatiel (it was my first pet), and I currently have a cat.

I have a background as a UX Designer, I worked in this profession for two years, it's something I like to do. I really liked the company and my work, but 2 weeks ago I took courage and quit my job to start my own agency. I came to Canada in search of a new point of view. I want to travel and live more.
